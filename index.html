About Tenjou Studio

Tenjou is an architectural studio based in Portugal, focused on creating minimal sustainable spaces in harmony with nature. We design with natural materials and modern techniques to achieve a balance between performance, comfort, and environmental responsibility.

Jacopo Marchesoni

Tenjou Studio was founded by Jacopo Marchesoni, an Italian interdisciplinary architect, who studied in Milan and Porto. His journey through diverse architectural cultures, combined with a deep interest in wood construction and interior design, led to the creation of Tenjou Studio, created in the Azores archipelago and deeply rooted in the islands' natural settings, now touching the coast of Porto.
