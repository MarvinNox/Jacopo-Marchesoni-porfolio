About

Tenjou studio is an architecture studio based
in the Azores, focused on creating simple, sustainable spaces
in harmony with nature. We design
with natural materials and modern techniques
to achieve a balance between performance, comfort,
and environmental responsibility.

Jacopo Marchesoni

Tenjou studio was founded by Jacopo Marchesoni, an Italian architect educated in Milan and Porto. His journey through diverse architectural cultures, combined with a deep interest in wood construction and sustainability, led to the creation of the studio in the Azores.
